Curtin University used Lectopia to record lectures and provide them to students for nearly a decade, but since the acquisition of Lectopia by Echo360 and the product's subsequent deprecation in favour of EchoSystem, Curtin no longer uses Lectopia.

The servers will be taken offline after December 31, 2013, and the fate of the data currently present on the legacy system is unknown, though it is unlikely that it will be made available to the public through other means. This is an archive of the lectures.
